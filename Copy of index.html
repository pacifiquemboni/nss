Itorero

Venue A, School Main Hall

Ibyo MUKARUSHEMA Eufrasie avuga arabeshya; umugabo we KARAMAGE Saveri witabye Imana akaba yari umuhungu wange ntiyigeze agura kiriya kibanza tuburana; ikibanza ninjye NYIRANGARUYE Asterie wakiguze mu mwaka wa 2012, nkigurishijwe na RYAGIZENDE mpita nubakamo nturamo, kuva icyo gihe kugeza ubu nimwo ntuye; amasezerano naguriyeho icyo kibanza yaratakaye, mpita mbimeneyesha ubuyobozi bw’umudugudu nabaturage; ariko mpagura hari abari bahari barimo na bamwe mu banyembibi nka NZABANTERURA Damien, NYIRAMANA Laurence, SIBOMANA Jean Bosco, n’abandi batari abanyembibi ndetse nuwanditse ayo masezerano naguriyeho icyo kibanza ariwe RUZIBIZA Celestin akaba na musaza wa MUKARUSHEMA Eufrasie; RYAGIZENDE amaze kungurisha yanampaye amasezerano nawe yaguriyeho icyo kibanza akigurishijwen’ uwitwa GIHOZA. Ikindi kimenyetso cyanjye ni umutangabuhamya witwa BANGIRA Theophille wari umwanditsi mu bugure bwa GIHOZA na RYAGIZENDE; Uyu BANGIRA Theophille akaba nanone yari mubatangabuhamya bari bahari RYAGIZENDE angurisha njyewe NYIRANGARUYE Asterie kiriya kibanza Ikindi kimenyetso nuko NTUYENABO Jean Claude wanditse amasezerano y’ amahimbano yerekanywa na MUKARUSHEMA Eufrasie abeshya ko KARAMAGE umugabo we waguze kiriya kibanza; uyu NTUYENABO jean claude ubwe yabaye umutangabuhamya mumasezerano y’ubugure bwa RUZIBIZA celestine na RYAGIZENDE, Ruzibiza Celestine agura ikibanza gihana imbibi n’ikibanza mburana na Mukarushema ; muri ayo masezerano handitswe ko ikibanza Ruzibiza Celestine aguze gihana imbibe nanjye NYIRANAGRUYE Asterie, NTUYENABO Jean Claude akaba yarasinye muri ayo masezerano nk’umuntu wari uhari muri ubwo bugure; kandi ubwo bugure bwanditswe n’ uwari umukuru w’umudugudu icyo gihe witwa BIMENYIMANA Anastase. Ikindi kimenyetso nuko uyu BIMENYIMANA Anastase wari umukuru w’umudugudu icyo gihe, wanditswe mumasezerano y’amahimbano ya KARAMAGE na RYAGIZENDE, we ubwe avuga ko ariya masezerano ntayo azi; Bityo, hashingiwe kuri ibi bimenyetso byose maze kugaragaza; ndasaba urukiko kwemeza ko kiriya kibanza ntuyemo ari icyanjye njyewe NYIRANGARUYE Asterie; rukemeza ko amasezerano y’ubugure bivugwako yabaye hagati ya RYAGIZENDE na KARAMAGE ari amahimbano dore ko kuri ayo masezerano Karamage bivugwa ko yaguze ntiyasinyeho, abatangabuhamya bayariho ntibasinye ndetse na Ntuyenabo jean claude uvuga ko ariwe mwanditsi wayo ntiyayasinyeho.
